 ETFs may have different ways to count fees, depending on your broker. There are three approaches:

1. On buy, the fee is added to your Average Buy Price, raising it up a bit. For reference, this is how Stocks are managed
2. The fee does not raise your ABP. It is instead added to your Tax Carryforward (Zainetto Fiscale) on buy
3. The fee does not raise your ABP. It is instead added to your Tax Carryforward when selling

**Note:** this choice won't obviously matter if the fees are 0.

[This article](https://www.capitalmente.it/perche-le-commissioni-degli-etf-generano-minusvalenze/) offers a more thorough explanation.
If you are unsure, ask your broker.
